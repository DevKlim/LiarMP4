The Research Problem

Traditional content moderation relies on Predictive AI (PredAI) analyzing engagement metadata or scalar probabilities. These models fail to catch "contextual malformation"â€”where the video is real, but the narrative and audio are deceptively recontextualized.

LiarMP4 investigates the efficacy of Generative AI (GenAI) and Multi-Agent Systems. Instead of looking at spread velocity, our agents recursively analyze the actual semantic dissonance between the pixels, waveforms, and claims.

  • Beyond tabular metadata analysis.
  • Evaluated against a rigorous Human-in-the-Loop Ground Truth.

Engineering-Grade Agentic Systems

Implementing robust software engineering principles to transition from stochastic "vibe-based" outputs to deterministic verification pipelines.

Agent-as-a-Proxy

Directly calling statistical models creates opaque black boxes. We wrap models in `AgentTool` classes, creating a conceptual wall that allows for pre-execution reasoning and post-execution validation by the ReAct orchestrator.

Fractal Chain-of-Thought

A recursive, multi-scale reasoning architecture. The agent forms a macro-hypothesis (Wide Aperture), decomposes it into meso-scale verifications (Visual/Audio branches), and synthesizes inter-scale consensus to prevent instruction drift.

Instruction Fidelity

Combating "Sequential Delegation Bugs" where orchestrators stop prematurely. We strictly enforce multi-turn persistence and mandate Rationale outputs to combat the "Hallucination of Alignment."

Veracity Vectors

Multi-dimensional 1-10 scoring framework quantifying Visual Integrity, Audio Forensics, Logical Consistency, and Source Credibility.

Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)

A dedicated browser extension and labeling studio workflow. Human intelligence calibrates the baseline Ground Truth to benchmark AI accuracy.

Google Cloud ADK Integration

Built atop the Agent Development Kit (ADK) utilizing the A2A (Agent-to-Agent) JSON-RPC protocol for seamless orchestration.
